FAQ's

Flights

To change your password please follow the steps below, your new password will be sent to your e-mail address that you entered when you first created your membership account.

  1. Log into my account on our website.
  2. You will find on the left side of the page "Change Password"
  3. Carry out the recommended steps then click "submit"
  4. The new password will be sent to your registered e-mail address immediately.

One of the following identification documents must be presented

  • Passport (must be available in all international flights)
  • Residency ID
  • National ID
